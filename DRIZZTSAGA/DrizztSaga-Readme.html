Introduction

This mod brings Drizzt and his companions into the game with their own major quest, also side quests many new areas, items (also ones to be forged) and creatures. Also two secret NPC’s for the evil alignment a bit into  the story.

Drizzt is on his way back to Icewind Dale from an errand in the Sword Coast, what he doesn't know is that dark times are upon the Dale and his companions will lead him and the protagonist into a adventure of epic proportions.

You meet Drizzt at his original area fighting some Gnolls. From there he departs on his errand until you meet him later. In Ulgoth’s Beard you will encounter Regis muttering something about a dark ranger. After meeting Drizzt again upon departing Durlag’s Tower after dispatching the Demon Knight the party sends him to the Inn at Ulgoth’s Beard. After joining Drizzt at the Inn, head back to Regis and the Drizzt Saga begins.

Installation

Extract Drizzt Saga into your game folder then run Setup-drizztsaga.exe. You need to start a new game, or the mod will not work.

To uninstall the mod: To uninstall/reinstall the mod, run Setup-drizztsaga.exe (found in your game folder), and choose the desired option.

*For Tutu/BGT you need to have TotSC and ToB installed.

*You need to start a new game.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How is this mod different that the original Drizzt Saga version from Blackwyrm’s Lair?
A: This version is modified so that it will launch after the party leaves Durlag’s Tower, instead of in the earlier chapters of the game. This allows the party to gain several levels and actually be able to participate in many of the quests instead of merely being observers due to being such a low level.

Q: Has any of the content from the original version changed.
A: No. Other than the required modifications for the mod launch, all content remains the same.
